Frog & Cog Creations was started by best friends Christina Rubenstein and Robyn Burdick. We are independent artists who love drawing, painting, crafting, and creating in general! We run our shop out of our apartment in a tiny town in rural Western Washington. We do a lot of fanart as well as original art, and it is always top quality! Make sure to take a look at our shop, peek at our Tumblr for new art pieces the second they're finished, and updates on our convention schedule!
